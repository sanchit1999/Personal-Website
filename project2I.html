INTERESTS



Coding

I developed interest in coding in class XI as I had taken Computer Science Course in my School. But I could understand its real importance only after JEE in terms of the problem-solving abilities one requires to excel in it. I started practicising on various competitive coding websites after JEE like codechef, codeforces which increased my exposure towards the subject.

Sports

I was interested in sports from the very beginning. I started playing hockey and table tennis in class VI th. I had interest in both the sports. In class VIII th, I played interzonals of TT in Delhi and played Delhi State for Hockey in class IX th. Besides these I also like swimming, football and running. I believe that one should always focus on his well physical being as it refreshes our mind and helps us improve further academics-wise.

Movies/Sitcoms

I am a lot into Hollywood movies and sitcoms. In particular, I like suspense thrillers,comical and action movies. I am a big fan of Jason Statham and follow his movies. I have completed various comical sitcoms and hope to continue watching them.
