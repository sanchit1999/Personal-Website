ABOUT ME

I was born and brought up in Delhi and currently reside in Hyderabad. I have a small family consisting of my father, my mother, me and my younger sister. I believe in hardwork and the need to work efficiently. I was guided by my parents throughout my life which inculcated some good values in me. Determination and self-confidence are two of the most important keywords that I rely upon.

I studied in Manav Sthali School in New Delhi and have a deep relationship with it. I would always be thankful to all the teachers who taught me something at any point of my life.

I am deeply inspired by quotes some of them being:

"Work so silently that your Success shouts" - Frank Ocean

"Quality is never an Accident, It is always a result of intelligent effort" - John Ruskin
